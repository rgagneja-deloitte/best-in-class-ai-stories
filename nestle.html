Built to prove value fast and scale with confidence

Design

Define the business problem, confirm user journeys, prioritize use cases, and align on data sources, metrics, and technical approach.

Build

Configure data pipelines, business rules, and interface components through iterative sprints with regular demos and feedback loops.

Test

Run accuracy testing, UAT, and responsible AI checks so the end-to-end experience works before the pilot starts.

Pilot

Deploy in a live business setting, capture efficiency and ROI, and use real user feedback to guide broader rollout.

Program shape

The program followed a 16-week shape with design up front, at least two build sprints, UAT before launch, and a minimum five-week pilot to validate real-world value.

Lessons learned

Engage end users in every stage, not just at launch

Real sales users and data SMEs helped shape use cases, prompts, outputs, and testing. That kept the product aligned to the way teams actually sell.

Treat data feasibility as a gate, not an afterthought

The team used structured data discovery early so use cases only moved forward when the required data and quality were actually available.

Lock design before major build work begins

Technical and functional design, including governance, should be aligned before the heavy build starts. That avoids rework and sprint churn later.

Prioritize accuracy and readiness over raw speed

The delivery approach emphasized rigorous testing and progressive integration. Accuracy came first, then scale and speed improvements followed.

Deliver capabilities in phases with lean governance

A minimal working flow was stood up early, then expanded use case by use case, with simple decision rights and stage gates to keep momentum without losing control.
